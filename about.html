FORM·A dsgn

Founded in 2017 in Kyiv, FORM·A dsgn started as a local architectural practice and has grown into an international design bureau. Our projects range from small cafés and private homes to large restaurants, medical facilities and full-scale architectural developments across Ukraine and beyond.

Every project starts with context: the site, the light, how the space will be used and who will use it. From there we work through proportion, materials and detail until the design feels considered rather than decorative.

We oversee every stage, from concept to realisation, with the same attention to precision throughout. With an office now in Bratislava, we continue expanding across Europe, working the same way we always have: building spaces that are functional, well-made and made to last.
